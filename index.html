Co-op Experience

SaskTel, Regina, SK

I worked as a Full-time Co-op student for 8 months in SaskTel. Being a "Technical Assistant (Advancing Technologies)", I got a valuable chance to work in several fields such as Machine Learning, LiDAR and some Software Development tasks. I worked on the following projects here:

SaskTel service churn model

Trained and tested several Machine Learning models (including Random Forest, Gradient Boosting Decision Trees and XGBoost classifier) to identify the responsible factors for a significant churn in a former SaskTel service. Here, XGBoost classifier surpassed all with 81.69% accuracy.

Natural Language Processing on Finance Credit Data

Trained and tested Flair and Spacy models for natural language processing so that it could provide valuable insights for better customer service and credit retention. They both achieved nearly 50% of F1-Score

LiDAR Data Classification

LiDAR data (of one city in Saskatchewan) was analyzed using Python and its LiDAR compatible libraries and then was classified into vegetation, buildings, water and ground surfaces.
